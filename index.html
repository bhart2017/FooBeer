About FooBeer!


Tradition since 2017

FooBeer! was created in 2017 by Ben, Keisha, and Jordan from GA Tech to pair great food with even greater beer. We set out on a journey to make sure the people knew what would pair well so our meals are more enjoyable.

Our mission is simple here at FooBeer! We provide great meals you can enjoy at home, when out, or if you're just curious; and we pair them with beer's that compliment your selection. Trust the food junkies from college, you'll definitely thank us later!
